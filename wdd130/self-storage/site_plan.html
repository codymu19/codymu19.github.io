Overview

Purpose

Do you have too much stuff in your house or apartment? Do you need an offsite place to store documents or tools? Are you moving and need to store things? I have a range of storage units that are available to store anything from boxes of toys to a car. All storage units are completely automated so you can access and pay for your storage all with just your phone. Come and enjoy 24/7 easy access and carefree self-storage units today!

Audience

Families that are changing housing. Local buisnesses that need storage for documents or tools. Locals, age 25+, that need extra space.

Home page

Message and action items of website. People will visit my website to find the size, cost, location, and accessibility of the storage units that the business offers. The sizes will be 5’ by 5’, 5’ x 10’, 10’ x 10’, 10’ x 15’, 10’ x 20’, 10’ x 25’, and 10’ x 30’. The cost will be about the square feet times $1.5. The location will be Pocatello, Idaho. The website will advertise the accessibility of the storage units including information about the easy payment system, easy access to unit from their smartphone. When visiting my website, visitors should be invited or directed to “Rent Now”, “Contact Us”, “Find a storage unit”, and to “Pay online”. Visitor will come to my site instead of another to get information or complete the advertised options because the website will be laid out to draw their attention to the action items and answers to their questions will be easy to find.

FAQ (Page 2)

Questions that need to be answered on site. People who visit the site will expect answers to questions such as what size unit I should get and what is available, cost of units, location of storage center, acceptable forms of payment, and contact information (office hours, access hours, phone number, email). The site will also mention and advertise what things they can store in the units.

Frequently Asked Questions. What are Office hours? Monday-Friday from 8:00am – 5:00pm Saturday from 9:00 am – 12:00pm Sunday CLOSED How to pay my bill? Pay here (link). You may pay your bill with a debit or credit card or by bank transfer. When are access hours? You can access your unit daily, 24 hours a day. What security is available? There is 24/7 surveillance on each of the storage units and all access if recorded through Noke smart keyless entry. Is there a minimum rental time? We rent month to month. When you move in the month will be prorated. When you move out you will be charged for the whole month Are moving carts and dollies provided? There will be moving carts and dollies available throughout the building Do I need to bring my own lock? You do not need your own lock. All unit have Noke smart keyless entry systems. What do I need to rent a storage unit? You need two forms of ID. Such as a driver’s license, passport, state ID What items are not allowed to be stored? Perishables, plants, animals, stolen or illegal goods, flammable or hazardous items, combustible items or liquids are not allowed to be stored. Do I need Storage Insurance? Insurance is not provided but you may purchase insurance from us or an outside vendor. Do I need to give a move out notice? Yes. Give a us a notice 15 days before you plan to move out. What sizes storage units are there? 5’ by 5’, 5’ x 10’, 10’ x 10’, 10’ x 15’, 10’ x 20’, 10’ x 25’, and 10’ x 30’ Is there a student or military discount? Yes. 10% off your monthly price for both student and military.
