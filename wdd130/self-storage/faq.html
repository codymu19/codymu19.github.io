Frequently Asked Questions

What are Office hours?

Monday-Friday from 8:00am – 5:00pm Saturday from 9:00 am – 12:00pm Sunday CLOSED

How to I pay my bill?

Pay here (link). You may pay your bill with a debit or credit card or by bank transfer.

When are access hours?

You can access your unit daily, 24 hours a day.

What security is available

There is 24/7 surveillance on each of the storage units and all access if recorded through Noke smart keyless entry.

Do I need storage insurance?

Insurance is not provided but you may purchase insurance from us or an outside vendor.

Do I need to give a move out notice?

Yes. Give us a notice 15 days before you plan to move out.

Is there a minimum rental time?

We rent from month to month. However, when you move in your first month will be prorated to the time that you move in. When you move out you will be charged for the whole month.

Are moving carts and dollies provided?

There will be moving carts and dollies available throughout the building

Do I need to bring my own lock?

You do not need your own lock. All unit have Noke smart keyless entry systems.

What do I need to rent a storage unit?

You need two forms of ID. Such as a driver’s license, passport, state ID.

What items are not allowed to be stored?

Perishables, plants, animals, stolen or illegal goods, flammable or hazardous items, combustible items or liquids are not allowed to be stored.

What size storage units are there?

5’ by 5’, 5’ x 10’, 10’ x 10’, 10’ x 15’, 10’ x 20’, 10’ x 25’, and 10’ x 30'.
